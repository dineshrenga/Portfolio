Application Development Stages

Analysis

An analysis is a detailed examination and interpretation of data, processes, or systems to gain insights, identify patterns, trends, and inform strategic decision-making effectively.

Design

Design is the process of creating a plan or solution for a specific purpose, blending aesthetics and functionality to meet user needs and project goals.

Development

Development is the process of building and implementing a solution, transforming designs or concepts into functional products, software, or systems through coding, construction, testing, or other methods.

Testing

Testing is the process of evaluating a system, product, or software to identify defects, ensure functionality, and verify that it meets specified requirements and quality standards.

Launching

Launching is the process of releasing a product, service, or system to the market or end users, marking its official availability after development, testing, and preparation.

Support

Support is the ongoing assistance provided to users or customers after a product, service, or system is launched, ensuring its proper functioning and addressing any issues or concerns.
